# Allianz Motor Breakdown Cover - Bronze

Bronze is our entry-level breakdown cover. It provides roadside assistance and local recovery within 10 miles when your vehicle breaks down away from home. Breakdowns at or near your home address are not included under Bronze.

## What is covered

### Mechanical or electrical failure at the roadside

If your vehicle suffers a sudden mechanical or electrical failure at the roadside - engine failure, starter motor or alternator problems, gearbox or clutch failure, or any other fault that prevents the vehicle from being driven safely - we will dispatch an engineer to attempt a repair. The repair attempt covers up to 30 minutes of labour at the scene. If the fault cannot be resolved within that time, we will arrange recovery to the nearest suitable garage, up to 10 miles. Parts and materials are at your own cost. This benefit applies only to breakdowns that occur more than a quarter of a mile from your registered home address.

### Battery failure and discharge

If your vehicle will not start or loses power because of a flat or discharged battery - whether from a battery that has failed over time, from leaving the lights or accessories on overnight, or from a battery no longer able to hold charge - we will attend and attempt a jump-start. If the vehicle cannot be driven after the attempt, we will recover it to the nearest suitable garage, up to 10 miles. Replacement batteries are at your own cost.

### Tyre damage and punctures

If you suffer a puncture, blowout, or tyre damage that makes the vehicle unsafe or undrivable - from road debris, nails, a kerbed tyre, or a sidewall failure - we will attend and fit your spare wheel at the roadside. If no roadworthy spare tyre is available, we will recover the vehicle to the nearest suitable tyre fitter or garage, up to 10 miles. The cost of replacement tyres is at your expense.

### Severe weather immobilisation

If your vehicle is immobilised by severe weather - including flooding or water ingress into the engine bay after driving through standing water, snow drifts, ice, or being stranded on a waterlogged road - we will attend to assess the situation. Where it is safe to do so, we will recover the vehicle clear of the hazard to the nearest suitable garage, up to 10 miles. Flood-damaged engines cannot be repaired at the roadside.

## What is not covered

### Commercial use and hire-or-reward

This policy does not cover any vehicle being used for hire-and-reward, rideshare, taxi, private hire, cab, or any other form of commercial passenger transport. If the vehicle is being used to carry paying passengers at the time of the incident - including through app-based platforms such as Uber or Lyft, or any licensed taxi or private hire operation - no cover is provided under this policy. This exclusion applies regardless of tier.

### Road traffic accidents and collisions

This policy covers sudden mechanical and electrical breakdown only. Incidents arising from road traffic accidents, collisions with another vehicle, impacts with stationary objects, animals, or leaving the carriageway are not covered. If your vehicle has been involved in a road traffic accident, please contact your motor insurance provider. Breakdown cover is not a substitute for a motor insurance claim.

### Lost, stolen, or locked-in keys

Lost keys, stolen keys, keys locked inside the vehicle, and lockout incidents are not classified as mechanical or electrical breakdowns and are not covered under any tier. We recommend contacting a locksmith directly for a lockout. If the incident is an electronic key fob fault or an immobiliser fault preventing the engine from starting, please describe it as an electrical fault so we can assess eligibility separately.
