ALLIANZ MOTOR BREAKDOWN COVER - GOLD PLUS (UK + EUROPEAN COVER)

Includes all Gold (Sections C-G) benefits, plus:

Section H - European Cover
Cover extends to breakdowns in the following countries: Austria, Belgium, Czech Republic, Denmark, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Ireland, Italy, Luxembourg, Netherlands, Norway, Poland, Portugal, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland.

European benefits:
- Roadside assistance and local recovery in the country of breakdown
- If vehicle cannot be repaired within 48 hours:
  - Vehicle repatriation to the UK, OR
  - Replacement vehicle for up to 14 days (Group A)
- Emergency accommodation: up to £100 per person per night, max 3 nights
- Return travel: economy air or standard rail fare to the UK for driver and passengers

European exclusions:
- Maximum vehicle age for European cover: 10 years
- Trip duration: cover applies for journeys of up to 90 days
- You must carry your policy number and our European assistance phone number

All UK exclusions from Gold also apply in Europe.
