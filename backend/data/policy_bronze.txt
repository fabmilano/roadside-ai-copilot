ALLIANZ MOTOR BREAKDOWN COVER - BRONZE (ROADSIDE ASSIST)

Section A - Eligibility
Covers private motor vehicles up to 3,500kg, not exceeding 15 years old.
Excludes: vehicles used for hire/reward, commercial transportation of persons (taxis, rideshare, private hire), driving instruction, rental, or emergency services.
Maximum 5 callouts per year across all cover levels.

Section B - Definitions
Breakdown: Sudden and unexpected mechanical, electrical, or electronic failure causing immobilisation.
Home: Your permanent UK residential address.
Roadside: Any public road in the UK more than 1/4 mile from your home address.

Section C - Roadside Assist
In the event of a breakdown more than 1/4 mile from your home address:
- We will attend the vehicle and attempt repair for up to 30 minutes labour.
- Parts and materials are at your cost.
- If repair is not possible within 30 minutes, we will arrange local recovery to the nearest suitable repairer.
- Local recovery is within 10 miles of the breakdown location.
- Passengers will be transported with the vehicle to the repairer.

NOT COVERED under Bronze:
- Breakdown at or within 1/4 mile of your home address
- Recovery beyond 10 miles
- Onward travel (hotel, car hire, rail fare)
- European cover
- Misfuelling
