ALLIANZ MOTOR BREAKDOWN COVER - SILVER

Includes all Bronze (Clauses C1-C4) benefits, plus Home Start (Clause D1).

Clause C1 - Mechanical or electrical failure at the roadside
@tiers: silver
@outcome: covered
@event_type: Roadside breakdown
@services: Roadside repair attempt (up to 30 minutes labour) | Local recovery to nearest suitable garage (up to 10 miles)

If your vehicle suffers sudden mechanical or electrical failure at the roadside — including engine failure, starter motor faults, alternator problems, gearbox failure, clutch failure, or any other immobilising mechanical or electronic fault — we will dispatch an engineer to attempt a repair at the scene. If the fault cannot be resolved within 30 minutes, we will arrange local recovery to the nearest suitable garage. Parts and materials are at your cost. Cover applies more than one quarter of a mile from your registered home address.

Clause C2 - Battery failure or discharge
@tiers: silver
@outcome: covered
@event_type: Flat battery (electrical failure)
@services: Jump-start attempt or battery diagnostic at the roadside | Local recovery to nearest garage if battery cannot be jump-started (up to 10 miles)

If your vehicle will not start or has lost power due to a discharged, flat, or failed battery — whether from dead cells, leaving lights or accessories on overnight, or a battery that no longer holds charge — we will attend and attempt a jump-start. If the battery cannot hold charge and the vehicle remains undrivable, we will recover it to the nearest suitable garage. Replacement batteries are at your cost.

Clause C3 - Tyre damage and punctures
@tiers: silver
@outcome: covered
@event_type: Flat tyre
@services: Roadside wheel change using your spare tyre | Local recovery to nearest tyre fitter if no spare is available (up to 10 miles)

If you suffer a puncture, blowout, or tyre damage that renders the vehicle unsafe or undrivable — caused by road debris, nails, a kerbed tyre, or a tyre sidewall failure — we will attend and fit your spare wheel at the roadside. If no roadworthy spare tyre is available, we will recover the vehicle to the nearest suitable tyre fitter or garage. The cost of replacement tyres is at your expense.

Clause C4 - Severe weather immobilisation
@tiers: silver
@outcome: covered
@event_type: Severe weather breakdown
@services: Roadside assessment and safe recovery from hazard | Local recovery to nearest suitable garage (up to 10 miles)

If your vehicle is immobilised by severe weather conditions — including flooding, water ingress into the engine bay after driving through standing water, snow drifts, ice, hail damage, or being stranded on a waterlogged or impassable road — we will attend to assess the vehicle. Where it is safe to do so, we will recover the vehicle clear of the hazard and to the nearest suitable garage. Repairs to flood-damaged vehicles or engines cannot be conducted at the roadside.

Clause D1 - Home Start
@tiers: silver, gold
@outcome: covered
@event_type: Home Start
@services: Home Start repair attempt (up to 30 minutes labour at or near home) | Local recovery from home to nearest suitable garage (up to 10 miles)

If your vehicle breaks down at or within one quarter of a mile of your registered home address — including on your own driveway, in your street outside your house, in a home car park, or just outside your property — we will attend and attempt a repair. If the vehicle cannot be fixed within 30 minutes, we will arrange recovery to the nearest suitable garage. This benefit extends cover to home breakdowns, which are excluded under Bronze. Cover is available 24 hours a day, seven days a week.
