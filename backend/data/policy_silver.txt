ALLIANZ MOTOR BREAKDOWN COVER - SILVER (ROADSIDE + HOME START)

Includes all Bronze (Section C) benefits, plus:

Section D - Home Start
In the event of a breakdown at or within 1/4 mile of your home address:
- We will attend the vehicle and attempt repair for up to 30 minutes labour.
- If repair is not possible, we will arrange local recovery to the nearest suitable repairer within 10 miles.

All other Bronze terms, exclusions, and limits apply.

NOT COVERED under Silver:
- National recovery beyond 10 miles
- Onward travel (hotel, car hire, rail fare)
- European cover
- Misfuelling
