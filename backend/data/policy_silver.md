# Allianz Motor Breakdown Cover - Silver

Silver extends Bronze with one additional benefit: Home Start. You are covered at or near your registered home address as well as at the roadside. All Bronze benefits apply in full.

## What is covered

### Mechanical or electrical failure at the roadside

If your vehicle suffers a sudden mechanical or electrical failure at the roadside - engine failure, starter motor or alternator problems, gearbox or clutch failure, or any other fault that prevents the vehicle from being driven safely - we will dispatch an engineer to attempt a repair for up to 30 minutes. If the fault cannot be resolved, we will arrange recovery to the nearest suitable garage within 10 miles. Parts and materials are at your own cost.

### Battery failure and discharge

If your vehicle will not start or loses power due to a flat or discharged battery - from a battery that has failed, from leaving the lights or accessories on, or from a battery unable to hold charge - we will attend and attempt a jump-start. If the vehicle cannot be driven after the attempt, we will recover it to the nearest suitable garage within 10 miles. Replacement batteries are at your own cost.

### Tyre damage and punctures

If you suffer a puncture, blowout, or tyre damage that makes the vehicle unsafe or undrivable, we will attend and fit your spare wheel at the roadside. If no roadworthy spare is available, we will recover the vehicle to the nearest tyre fitter or garage within 10 miles. Replacement tyres are at your own expense.

### Severe weather immobilisation

If your vehicle is immobilised by severe weather - flooding, water ingress into the engine bay after driving through standing water, snow drifts, ice, or being stranded on a waterlogged road - we will attend and, where safe, recover the vehicle to the nearest suitable garage within 10 miles. Flood-damaged engines cannot be repaired at the roadside.

### Home Start

If your vehicle breaks down at or within a quarter of a mile of your registered home address - including on your own driveway, in your street, or in a nearby car park - we will attend and attempt a repair. If the vehicle cannot be fixed, we will arrange recovery to the nearest suitable garage within 10 miles. Home Start extends cover to breakdowns that occur at home, which are excluded under Bronze. This benefit is available 24 hours a day, seven days a week.

## What is not covered

### Commercial use and hire-or-reward

This policy does not cover any vehicle being used for hire-and-reward, rideshare, taxi, private hire, or any form of commercial passenger transport. If the vehicle is carrying paying passengers at the time of the incident - including via Uber, Lyft, or any licensed private hire operation - no cover is provided. This exclusion applies across all tiers.

### Road traffic accidents and collisions

This policy covers sudden mechanical breakdown only. Collisions, impacts, and damage caused by road traffic accidents are not covered. Please contact your motor insurance provider for accident-related incidents.

### Lost, stolen, or locked-in keys

Lost keys, stolen keys, and lockout incidents are not classified as breakdowns and are not covered. Contact a locksmith directly. An electronic key fob fault or immobiliser fault preventing the engine from starting may be assessed as an electrical fault if described as such.
