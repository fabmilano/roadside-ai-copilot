ALLIANZ MOTOR BREAKDOWN COVER - GOLD (ROADSIDE + HOME START + NATIONAL RECOVERY + ONWARD TRAVEL)

Includes all Silver (Sections C + D) benefits, plus:

Section E - UK Recovery
If the vehicle cannot be repaired at the roadside or at your home:
- We will arrange and pay for recovery of the vehicle and up to 7 passengers to any single UK destination of your choice.
- This replaces the 10-mile local recovery limit in Bronze/Silver.
- Labour time extended to up to 60 minutes before recovery is arranged.

Section F - Onward Travel
If the vehicle cannot be repaired same-day, you are entitled to ONE of the following:
- Replacement vehicle: Group A hire car for up to 24 hours
- Hotel accommodation: One night for driver and passengers, up to £150 total
- Alternative transport: Standard class rail fare for driver and passengers to their original destination, up to £75 per person

Section G - Additional Benefits (Gold)
- Misfuelling: If you put the wrong fuel in your vehicle, we will arrange draining, flushing, and recovery. Fuel replacement is at your cost.
- Message relay: We will contact one person on your behalf to advise of your situation.

NOT COVERED under Gold:
- European breakdown
- Vehicles used for hire/reward or commercial passenger transport
- Vehicles over 15 years old
- Pre-existing faults known before the journey commenced
- Routine maintenance, servicing, or MOT failures
