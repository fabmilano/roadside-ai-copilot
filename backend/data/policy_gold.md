# Allianz Motor Breakdown Cover - Gold

Gold is our most comprehensive tier. It includes all Bronze and Silver benefits, extends local recovery to national recovery (any UK destination), and adds three Gold-only benefits: onward travel when the vehicle cannot be driven, misfuelling cover, and national-distance Home Start.

## What is covered

### Mechanical or electrical failure at the roadside

If your vehicle suffers a sudden mechanical or electrical failure at the roadside - engine failure, starter motor or alternator problems, gearbox or clutch failure, or any other fault that prevents safe driving - we will dispatch an engineer for up to 60 minutes of repair labour. If the fault cannot be resolved, we will arrange national recovery to any single UK destination of your choice: your home, your preferred garage, or your intended destination. Up to 7 passengers may travel with the vehicle. Parts are at your own cost.

### Battery failure and discharge

If your vehicle will not start or loses power due to a flat or discharged battery - dead cells, leaving accessories on, a battery that no longer holds charge, or an EV high-voltage battery management fault - we will attend and attempt a jump-start or full battery diagnostic. If the vehicle cannot be driven, we will recover it to any single UK destination of your choice. Replacement batteries and parts are at your own cost.

### Tyre damage and punctures

If you suffer a puncture, blowout, or tyre damage that renders the vehicle unsafe or undrivable, we will attend and fit your spare wheel at the roadside. If no roadworthy spare is available, we will recover the vehicle to any single UK destination of your choice - home, a tyre fitter, or your intended destination. Replacement tyres are at your own cost.

### Severe weather immobilisation

If your vehicle is immobilised by severe weather - flooding, water ingress into the engine bay after driving through standing water, snow drifts, ice, or being stranded on a waterlogged or impassable road - we will attend and arrange recovery to any single UK destination of your choice, including home or a specialist garage. Flood-damaged engines cannot be repaired at the roadside.

### Home Start

If your vehicle breaks down at or within a quarter of a mile of your registered home address, we will attend and attempt a repair. If the vehicle cannot be fixed, we will arrange national recovery to any UK destination of your choice. Gold Home Start is not restricted to local recovery, unlike Silver.

### Onward travel when the vehicle cannot be driven

If your Gold-covered vehicle cannot be repaired on the same day and is not drivable, you are entitled to one onward travel option of your choice:

- A Group A replacement hire car for up to 24 hours
- Hotel accommodation for one night for the driver and all passengers, up to GBP 150 in total
- Standard-class rail fares for all passengers to their intended destination, up to GBP 75 per person

Choose whichever option best suits your circumstances. This benefit applies when the vehicle is confirmed as not drivable at the time of the incident.

### Misfuelling

If you have put the wrong fuel type into your vehicle - diesel into a petrol engine, or petrol into a diesel - we will arrange a professional fuel drain and flush at the roadside or the nearest suitable garage. If the vehicle cannot be driven safely after the procedure, we will arrange onward recovery. The cost of replacing the fuel is at your own expense. Misfuelling cover is a Gold-only benefit and is not available under Bronze or Silver.

## What is not covered

### Commercial use and hire-or-reward

This policy does not cover any vehicle being used for hire-and-reward, rideshare, taxi, private hire, or any form of commercial passenger transport. If the vehicle is carrying paying passengers at the time of the incident - including via Uber, Lyft, or any licensed private hire or taxi operation - no cover is provided under any tier.

### Road traffic accidents and collisions

This policy covers sudden mechanical breakdown only. Accidents, collisions, and damage caused by impact are not covered. Please contact your motor insurance provider for accident-related incidents.

### Lost, stolen, or locked-in keys

Lost keys, stolen keys, and lockout incidents are not classified as breakdowns and are not covered. Contact a locksmith directly. An electronic key fob or immobiliser fault preventing engine start may be assessed as an electrical fault if described as such.
